Hello! I'm a graduate student at ISAE-ENSMA studying Aeronautical Mechanics and Energetics with a Propulsion major. I have previously studied Physics from St. Xavier's College, Kolkata. My broad research interests lie in aerodynamics and propulsion.

In general, I enjoy working on problems that involve understanding how complex systems behave and love to develop ways to model, analyze, and improve them. Over the past few years, I have worked on projects ranging from mechanical design and aerodynamic analysis to low-energy trajectory design and optimal control. One could say from my experiences that I have a particular interest in design-oriented engineering, where physical intuition, mathematical modeling and computational skills are equally important in solving real-world problems.
